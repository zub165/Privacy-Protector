Privacy Policy for Privacy Vault PRO

Effective Date: April 8, 2025

Privacy Vault PRO is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Data We Collect

None. Privacy Vault PRO does not collect or transmit any personal data. All user data is stored locally on the device and never leaves it.

2. Data Usage

We do not access, use, or share your personal data. The app runs completely offline and does not use third-party analytics, ads, or cloud syncing.

3. Permissions

The app may request access to biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) for secure access. This data is handled entirely by your device and never stored by us.

4. Security

Your data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption and remains solely on your device. We have no access to your vault contents or passwords.
